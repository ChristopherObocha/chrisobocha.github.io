My name is Chris and I'm in love with nature

I am recent graduate of Computer Science, from the prestigious Babcock University. I was born and I live in one of the poorer areas of Lagos, however, I am blessed to have attended one of the most expensive universities in Nigeria and this duality exposed me to one of the most unique experiences in my life and it is a major proponent in my ambitions to connect people of different backgrounds and to improve life for all through tech.

My Drive

Traversing through the dualities of rich and poor, I developed a strong interest in wealth gaps and how it affects or its effects on cultural and economic differences. Moved by my experiences, I constantly try to infuse my love for differing fields like history, geography, media to my love for tech. I decided to major in Computer Science as my first degree because I believe in foundational knowledge and I believe that tech is the future and positively harnessing its capabilities can greatly benefit humanity.

My goals

I propose the following objectives. First, I want to gain firm knowledge of applying the engineering principles to software development. Secondly, I want to learn processes, methodologies, tools which will allow me to deliver tech in impactful ways to society. Finally, I want to obtain the practical ability to manage projects and communication skills to build strong relationships with clients and the world in general.

To achieve my goals, it is imperative that I acquire a profound knowledge of innovation and management while developing both professional and research skills.
